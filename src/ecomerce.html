What is eCommerce store?

Today, about 25% of world trade takes place over the Internet. eCommerce includes the sale and exchange of goods and services online. eCommerce also encompasses areas such as online auctions, internet banking, payment gateways, and online tickets.

The key benefits of
eCommerce are:

Global market

The ability to expand the geography of your brand. You need not be limited to one city or country, the entire world is your market.

24/7 availability

An online store allows you to increase your sales opportunities. Regardless of business hours, eCommerce businesses can serve customers 24/7/365.

Reduced costs

Since you need not hire employees, pay rent, you can significantly reduce organizational costs.

Inventory Management

Automation of inventory management through the use of electronic tools will speed up the ordering, delivery and payment procedures.

Targeted marketing

By communicating directly with their customers, online stores shape their marketing efforts to provide better service to their customers.
